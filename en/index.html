Features built for everyday life

Real-time location

See active routes and vehicles on a clean, responsive map. Positions refresh continuously, so waiting feels… more predictable.

Smart schedules

Compare scheduled times with real-world status at a glance. Pick a route, a direction, a stop. That’s it.

Your network, under control

Route status, destinations, active vehicles, and ETAs — all in one place. Quick search and a natural map ↔ list flow.

Service alerts

Roadworks, incidents, weather, breakdowns… everything is grouped in one place, so you can understand what’s happening before it surprises you.

My Bus History

Follow the fleet: vehicle number, status, last route, and model. Great for enthusiasts — and useful for network teams, too.

Personalization

Automatic geolocation, instant map access, language selection, and more. Set up the app the way you like to travel.
